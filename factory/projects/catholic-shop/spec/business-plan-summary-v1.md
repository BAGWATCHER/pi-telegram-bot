# Catholic Marketplace — Business Plan Summary
Date: 2026-05-01

## Model: Provenance-First Catholic Marketplace

### What We Are
A mobile-first marketplace that aggregates unique Catholic devotional items from gift shops, monasteries, and shrines at holy sites around the world. Organized by destination, powered by provenance.

### What We're Not
- Another generic Catholic gift store
- A dropship random-rosary shop
- Amazon for Catholic stuff
- A warehouse-heavy inventory operation

### The Wedge
**Nobody connects the travel inspiration to the purchase.** Catholics dream of Lourdes, save for Jerusalem, watch YouTube videos of Fatima. But when they want to buy something authentically from those places, they get SEO spam or random Etsy. We close that gap.

### Target Customer
- Catholic women 25-55 (primary gift buyers)
- Pilgrims planning trips (pre-purchase excitement)
- Pilgrims who visited and want more (post-trip connection)
- Gift buyers for sacraments (First Communion, Confirmation, weddings)
- Catholic converts (building devotional life)

### Revenue Model (Marketplace-Light)
- Shops list items → we curate and publish
- Customer orders → shop fulfills
- We take 20% commission
- No inventory, no warehouse, no shipping

### Unit Economics (Target)
```
Avg order value: $45
Commission: 20% = $9/order
Customer acquisition: $5 (organic/social first)
Net per order: $4
Orders/day target v1: 20 → $80/day → $2.4K/mo
Orders/day target v2: 100 → $400/day → $12K/mo
```

### Sourcing Funnel
1. Identify shops (Google Maps, pilgrimage YouTube, Catholic travel blogs)
2. Contact (email/DM/WhatsApp — simple pitch)
3. Onboard (form → catalog upload → curation)
4. Publish (provenance-verified → live on marketplace)
5. Fulfill (order → shop ships → tracking relayed)

### Key Metrics
- Destinations live: target 10 at launch
- Products live: target 100 at launch
- Partner shops: target 20 at launch
- Mobile conversion rate: target 2%+
- Provenance-verified %: target 100%

### Risks & Mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Shops don't respond to outreach | Start with English-speaking + digitally-active shops; use pilgrim ambassadors |
| International shipping is slow/expensive | Set expectations clearly; build shipping calculator; offer free shipping threshold |
| Quality inconsistent | Curation gate; provenance verification; review system |
| Stripe/processing for international shops | We handle payments; shops get paid after fulfillment |
| Catholic market too niche to scale | Catholic population 1.3B; online Catholic spend growing; plus gift market is massive |

### Competitive Moat
- **Provenance data** — once we build relationships with 50+ monastery/shrine shops, it's hard to replicate
- **Destination content** — SEO for "rosary from Lourdes" / "Fatima gift" etc.
- **Trust** — verified shop badges, provenance transparency
- **Network effects** — more shops → more destinations → more customers → more shops
