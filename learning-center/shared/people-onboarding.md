# People Onboarding (Learning Center)

## What this gives you

- A single place to see what we learned
- Source-linked notes instead of scattered chat history
- Repeatable training loops you can run daily

## How to use it in 5 minutes

1. Open `learning-center/index.md`
2. Open your track under `learning-center/tracks/`
3. Read `context.md` first
4. Pick one drill from `training-loop.md`
5. Ship one artifact and log one note

## What to log after each session

- What you built
- What failed
- What changed in your mental model
- What to test next
