# Question Bank (derived from video)

Use for mock interviews and agent training.

## Primitive-level questions

1. What is agentic RAG, and where does it fail? ([11:54]–[12:01])
2. What is metadata-filtered RAG, and when does it matter? ([11:56])
3. How do you choose a vector database for your RAG system? ([11:58])
4. What is the difference between an agent and a graph in system behavior? ([12:03]–[12:05], [21:21])
5. What is an eval in your stack? What is scored? ([12:07], [22:15])
6. How do you design an eval set and judge quality drift over time? ([23:21]–[23:36])
7. What is your eval pipeline from model change to deployment decision? ([23:38]–[23:57])

## Workflow questions

8. Walk through your coding-agent methodology from ticket to shipped code. ([10:52]–[10:58], [15:20])
9. How do you progressively disclose context to avoid context collapse? ([16:20])
10. Which skills/tooling or MCP integrations do you use, and why? ([14:40], [15:58], [21:59])
11. How do you ensure generated code quality before merge? ([17:00], [42:11])
12. What are your non-negotiable quality gates? ([34:48], [35:16], [47:09])

## Credibility + communication questions

13. Open your repo and explain one hard decision under constraints. ([29:48], [37:54])
14. Show one failure and how you corrected it with evidence. ([37:05]–[37:10])
15. Explain your project so both PM and engineering audiences can follow. ([27:34], [30:52], [41:50])
16. Why should a hiring manager trust your signal over resume noise? ([07:10], [08:26], [25:15])

## Red-team follow-ups

17. If your RAG answers degrade, what telemetry tells you first?
18. If your agent starts hallucinating tool calls, what rollback path exists?
19. If eval scores improve but user outcomes worsen, what hypothesis do you test?
20. If you had to remove one component (RAG, graph, agent, eval), what breaks first and why?
