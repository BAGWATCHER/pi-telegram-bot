# Track: Gauntlet AI

Purpose: mirror Gauntlet-style AI engineering training and convert it into reusable training for agents + humans.

## Track files

- `curriculum-map.md` — what they teach and in what sequence
- `training-loop.md` — repeatable daily/weekly execution loop
- `resources.md` — curated source list
- `context.md` — generated context bundle (rebuild via script)
- `video-studies/` — per-video deep dives (transcripts, study guides, question banks)

## Outcomes we care about

1. Ship production-capable AI systems quickly.
2. Demonstrate context engineering discipline.
3. Use evals + quality gates instead of vibe checks.
4. Train others through clear modules and rubrics.
