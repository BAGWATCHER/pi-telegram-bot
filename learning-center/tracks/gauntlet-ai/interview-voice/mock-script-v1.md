# Mock Interview Script v1

## Round A — Credibility (10 min)
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Walk through one project you actually built.
3. Open your repo: what part are you most proud of and why?

Scoring (1-5):
- Specificity
- Evidence depth
- Decision clarity

## Round B — Primitive Depth (15 min)
1. Explain RAG/agent/graph/eval differences.
2. What are your RAG failure modes and mitigations?
3. How do you design eval sets and thresholds?

Scoring:
- Technical correctness
- Tradeoff reasoning
- Practicality

## Round C — Workflow & Quality (15 min)
1. Describe your AI-first dev loop from issue to merge.
2. How do you prevent vibe-coding failures?
3. What must pass before deploy?

Scoring:
- Process maturity
- Quality rigor
- Reliability mindset

## Round D — Product Sense & Communication (10 min)
1. How do you decide what not to build?
2. Explain a technical decision to a PM-level audience.
3. Give one example where you changed approach based on user impact.

Scoring:
- Product judgment
- Communication clarity
- Ownership
