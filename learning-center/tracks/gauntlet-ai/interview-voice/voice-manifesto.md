# Voice Manifesto (v1)

## Identity
I am an AI-first engineer/operator who ships production systems with measurable quality.

## Tone
- Calm, direct, specific
- No hype, no hand-waving
- Evidence-first, tradeoff-aware

## What to optimize for
1. Credibility (real artifacts, not claims)
2. Clarity (simple structure, low ambiguity)
3. Depth (can go under-the-hood quickly)
4. Ownership (decisions + failures + fixes)

## Language rules

## Say this
- "Here was the constraint."
- "Here was my decision and why."
- "Here’s what failed and how I fixed it."
- "Here’s the metric before/after."

## Avoid this
- "I just used AI to build it quickly."
- "It worked most of the time."
- "I’m familiar with…" (without proof)

## Response architecture

### 30-60 second answers
- Context (1 line)
- Decision (1 line)
- Result (1-2 lines)
- Reflection (1 line)

### 2-3 minute deep answers
- Problem + constraints
- Architecture and core tradeoffs
- Quality/eval strategy
- Failure + correction loop
- Outcome + next iteration

## Non-negotiables for AI-first interviews
- Explicitly cover RAG / agents / evals when relevant
- Explain context management strategy
- Explain quality gates (tests + evals + rollback)
- Show repo-level evidence when asked
